How to Navigate the
Open Data Portal

If this is your first time seeking open data, navigating the portal can be confusing. We've boiled the process down to three easy steps to get you started so you can find the information you're looking for faster.

Understand the lingo

The volume of information available requires us to get specific, impacting how you will search for certain data. You'll need know the keywords to narrow down your search results. Before clicking the search bar, spend a minute reviewing our Glossary of Terms to see how your data might be categorized

Navigate our
dataset catalog

In the dataset catalog, you can explore data by department, provided categories or using your own keywords—all of which will provide search results available for download. If the resulting data isn't what you were looking for, adjust your search and try again. Need to see this in action? Check out our blog to walk through a sample search.

Preview and
download the data

Once you’ve found a dataset that you're interested in, you can preview the data right on the page to confirm it has the information you're looking for. Now that you’ve found your desired dataset, you can download it for free using a preferred format that matches your computer's capabilities. The data is now yours to work with!

Monitoring of Indicator Bacteria in Drinking Water

Last modified: 2020-05-18

Here are individual test results from the water monitoring database, plus the monthly totals reported to the state to demonstrate MCL compliance. This dataset does not include results of tests for chemical parameters.

Streets Repair Projects

Last modified: 2020-05-18

Repair projects include City streets that have been repaired since July 1, 2013 (first full fiscal year comprehensive street repair data is readily available) and those that are currently scheduled for repair.

Parking Meters Transactions

Last modified: 2020-05-17

All transactions for all parking meters. The raw datasets are large and may be difficult to work with. Aggregated versions are also available.Please note that data for 2019 is missing transactions for some days. We are working to get complete data.

Streets Repair Line Segments

Last modified: 2020-05-12

Shapefile of the City’s street network. This shapefile contains the street segments identified in our streets repair projects and Overall Condition Index datasets. This data is displayed on streets.sandiego.gov{:target=”_blank” rel="noopener”}.

Park Locations

Last modified: 2020-05-12

Properties managed by the City of San Diego Parks & Recreation Department.

Library Locations

Last modified: 2020-04-28

Point feature layer of City of San Diego library locations with associated website and contact information, created by the County of San Diego Department of Public Works GIS, in conjunction with San Diego County Library (SDCL).

City Owned Properties - Leases

Last modified: 2020-04-12

Data about leases for City-owned property, including lessee, rent amount and how the property is used.

City Owned Properties - Parcels

Last modified: 2020-04-10

Site codes for City-owned property in relation to each parcel that was donated or sold to the City. City sites as identified by site code may comprise multiple parcels.

City Owned Properties - Details

Last modified: 2020-04-10

Data about property the City of San Diego owns, including the cost, month and year of purchase and the acreage and usage of the site.

Parking Meters Locations

Last modified: 2019-09-23

Locations of parking meters with corresponding parking zones and areas.

Joint use parks

Last modified: 2019-09-17

Joint use facilities are intended to provide active and passive recreational opportunities for school children when school is in session and the general public when school is not in session.

Right-of-way parcels

Last modified: 2019-09-17

This is a subset of a countywide parcels dataset maintained by SanGIS. Right-of-way parcels are those that are legally dedicated to unhindered public travel. SanGIS uses county, state and federal sources to update this data.

Streetlight point locations

Last modified: 2017-05-03

The City has more than 60,000 outdoor lights, which include streetlights, pedestrian lighting, sports lighting, parking lot lighting and specialty lighting, the majority of which are Induction lights that emit an energy efficient broad spectrum light source. This dataset includes coordinates for each light.

Monitoring of Select Chemical Parameters in Drinking Water

Last modified: 2017-02-17

Results of tests on drinking water for alkalinity, color, turbidity, hardness, conductivity, total dissolved solids, and for selected anions.

Streets Overall Condition Index (OCI)

Last modified: 2017-01-17

The City performs street condition surveys and assigns an Overall Condition Index (OCI) number to each street. OCI is only one of many factors to determine the order of street repairs. OCI is not available for private or military streets or streets the City was unable to survey due to closures. This data is displayed on streets.sandiego.gov.

Street Sweeping Schedule

Last modified: 2016-07-05

This schedule includes the time and date for street sweeping by block. The City’s goal is to sweep residential streets at least once per month and commercial streets at least once per week.

Drinking Water Sample Sites

Last modified: 2016-07-05

Locations of sampling sites for the drinking water system. These locations exist to fulfill the federal Total Coliform Rule.

The way we get around has a significant impact on our pocketbook, the environment, and contributes to the well-being and quality of life of San Diegans. With the goal of creating a cleaner San Diego for future generations, the City of San Diego’s Climate Action Plan calls for helping more people bike, walk, or take transit to get around the city. To help track our progress towards these goals, the City is conducting a survey to understand how people commute to and from work. Thank you in advance for your participation!

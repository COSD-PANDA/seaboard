The way that we get around has a significant impact on climate change, and contributes to the well-being and quality of life of San Diegans. With the goal of creating a cleaner San Diego for future generations, the City of San Diego’s Climate Action Plan (CAP) calls for eliminating half of all greenhouse gas emissions by 2035, and helps achieve the greenhouse gas reduction targets set forth by the state of California. To help track our progress towards these goals, the City is conducting a commuter mode share survey.

Please take a moment to complete this survey. Your responses to the questions are voluntary. Any questions left unmarked will be recorded as ‘No Opinion.’ Thank you for your time!
